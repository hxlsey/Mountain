Welcome to GreenView Mountain Hotel

Tucked against the scenic slopes of Mount Arca, GreenView Mountain Hotel is the premier destination in Seattle for weddings, holiday vacations and weekend getaways.

Rooms and suites

Whether you want to stay in one of our fine guest rooms or rent out a suite for the entire family, we have space for you. Each of our 120 luxurious guest rooms and suites blend perfectly with the stunning natural beauty of the Mountains. We also offer guest comforts such as customized bath products, thick comforters, memory foam mattresses, closet space, HD flat screen TVs and a spectacular view of the mountains from the rooms.

Weddings

The GreenView Hotel is the perfect place for your destination wedding. About 200 acres of greenery and mountains, our hotel features stunning settings for indoor and outdoor ceremonies, receptions, and rehearsal dinners.
Wine & Dine
No matter what you crave, we have something to satisfy at GreenView. Our indoor-outdoor restaurant comes with an exquisite view of the greenery surrounding Mount Arca. Let our experienced culinary team take your taste buds on an adventure.
Spa
Relax and refresh with any one of our services at our spa. When you need to reset your mood, you can treat yourself and allow the expert and gentle professionals at our spa to perform facials, massages, hand and body care, and body treatments.
